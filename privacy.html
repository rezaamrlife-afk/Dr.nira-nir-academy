Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

Dr. NIRA ("the App") is an AI-powered PhD research advisor developed by NIR Academy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Dr. NIRA application.

By using Dr. NIRA, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

Dr. NIRA is designed with privacy in mind. The majority of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Research profile — name, university, degree level, field of study
  • Research proposals — all proposal sections and content you create
  • Thesis projects — thesis chapters and content
  • Knowledge Base — content extracted from uploaded documents
  • Citations library — saved academic references
  • Reading list — saved academic papers
  • Research gaps — extracted gap statements
  • Writer documents — academic writing drafts

All of the above data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers.

3. AI Processing

When you use AI-powered features (proposal generation, analysis, topic generation, etc.), the following data is sent to our AI processing infrastructure:

  • The specific text or content you submit for processing
  • Your research topic and field context
  • Extracted content from uploaded documents (for analysis features)

This data is processed by Groq API (via our secure Vercel serverless proxy) and is not stored permanently by NIR Academy after processing is complete.

4. Third-Party Services

Dr. NIRA uses the following third-party services:

  • Groq API — AI language model processing
  • OpenAlex API — Academic literature search (no personal data sent)
  • arXiv API — Academic paper search (no personal data sent)
  • Google Fonts — Typography (standard font loading)
  • Vercel — Application hosting and serverless functions

5. Data Storage & Security

Your research data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data remains on your device and is not synced to our servers
  • Clearing your browser data will permanently delete your Dr. NIRA data
  • Data is not accessible to other users or third parties
  • We recommend regularly exporting important work using the Export feature

6. Children's Privacy

Dr. NIRA is designed for PhD researchers and graduate students (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data at any time using the "Reset Data" feature in the Dashboard
  • Export your data before deletion using the Export feature
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns
